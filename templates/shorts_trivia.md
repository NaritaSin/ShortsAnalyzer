# YouTube Shorts Trivia Script Template

Use this structure for every trivia Short. Keep the total runtime within the channel's recommended duration.

## Hook

- Open with a surprising fact or question in the first 3 seconds.
- Grab attention immediately; do not start with a slow introduction.

## Introduction

- Briefly state what the viewer will learn.
- Set context in one or two short sentences.

## Main Story

- Present the core trivia with clear, paced narration.
- Use concrete details, numbers, or comparisons when possible.
- Build curiosity and keep momentum until the payoff.

## Ending

- Deliver the key takeaway or twist.
- Leave the viewer with a memorable fact or insight.

## CTA

- Encourage likes, comments, or follows with a short call to action.
- Optionally tease the next video or related topic.
